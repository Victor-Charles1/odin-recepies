Ingredients

  • Uncooked rice - 1 cup
  • Water - 2cups

Steps

  1. Pour water into pot and place on stove on high to boil
  2. Once water is boiling place Uncooked rice in pot
  3. stir and place lid on pot
  4. set stove on low heat
  5. leave alone for 20 minutes
  6. turn off stove you have succesfully boiled rice
